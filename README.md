# Gadget Sales Performance Dashboard 🔌

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global gadget sales, track store performance, and understand customer purchasing behavior across different regions.

## 📊 Dashboard Preview
<img width="1128" height="642" alt="Screenshot 2026-06-24 111652" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/34fce748-0174-44ae-a39c-73958105365b" />


## 💡 Key Insights & Features
* **Core Business Metrics:** Tracked $4.38M in total sales across 1,500 unique customers and 4 major store locations.
* **Product Distribution:** Evaluated sales volume by product types (Tablets leading sales volume at 3K units, followed by Laptops, Printers, and Monitors).
* **Advanced Visualizations:** * Used a **Treemap** to dissect the intersection of quantity sold by both Store Location and Region.
  * Implemented a **Sankey Diagram** to map the flow of total sales across different products and regions dynamically.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Analyzed the split between Retail and Wholesale customers using a donut chart breakdown.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & DAX Concepts
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Transformations:** Excel (Data cleaning and column splitting)
* **Visuals Used:** Treemap, Sankey Chart, Funnel/Bar breakdown, Donut chart, KPI Cards.

# International Coffee Sales Analytics ☕

A multi-year sales analysis dashboard tracking customer demographics and quantity purchased across global regions (United States, Ireland, and United Kingdom) from 2019 to 2022.

## 📊 Dashboard Preview
<img width="1131" height="642" alt="Screenshot 2026-06-24 111716" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/26da9283-2d65-430d-9725-a489f9565220" />


## 💡 Key Insights & Features
* **Geographic Breakdown:** Identified the United States as the primary market driver using pie chart and bar chart distributions across cities (with Washington and New York City leading in customer acquisition).
* **Trend Analysis:** Tracked volume growth year-over-year, peaking in 2021 with 1,143 units sold.
* **Product Variety Tracking:** Monitored 4 distinct coffee bean types (Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, Robusta) with an average unit price of $13.05.
* **Advanced Visualizations:** Utilized a **Sankey Diagram** to visualize how the preference for different coffee bean types shifted year-over-year from 2019 to 2022.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & DAX Concepts
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Transformations:** Geographic data grouping, date dimension mapping.
* **Features:** Slicers for country-level deep dives, Custom Ribbon/Sankey charts for temporal product tracking.
