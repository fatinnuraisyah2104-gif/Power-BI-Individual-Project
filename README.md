# Gadget Sales Performance Dashboard 🔌

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global gadget sales, track store performance, and understand customer purchasing behavior across different regions.

## 💡 Key Insights & Features
* **Core Business Metrics:** Tracked $4.38M in total sales across 1,500 unique customers and 4 major store locations.
* **Product Distribution:** Evaluated sales volume by product types (Tablets leading sales volume at 3K units, followed by Laptops, Printers, and Monitors).
* **Advanced Visualizations:** * Used a **Treemap** to dissect the intersection of quantity sold by both Store Location and Region.
  * Implemented a **Sankey Diagram** to map the flow of total sales across different products and regions dynamically.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Analyzed the split between Retail and Wholesale customers using a donut chart breakdown.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & DAX Concepts
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Transformations:** Excel (Data cleaning and column splitting)
* **Visuals Used:** Treemap, Sankey Chart, Funnel/Bar breakdown, Donut chart, KPI Cards.
